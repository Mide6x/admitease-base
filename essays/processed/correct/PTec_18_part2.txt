Although WordPress is not the sole blogging software available, it is a popular choice for several reasons: it's free, user-friendly, open-source, and continuously improved by a large community. There are many online resources, like Guido Stiehle ("The Jungle Marketer"), who provide free tutorials, including video guides, on setting up and utilizing your blog. You can easily find more tutorials by searching "how to use WordPress" online.
By investing some time in research, you'll realize why a blog is a practical option for establishing your online presence.
