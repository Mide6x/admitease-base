Nokia Unveils Mobile Computer
In response to customers' continued yearning for improved and quality products, the world-leading manufacturer of mobile phones, Nokia, unveiled a mobile computer, the Nokia N97 Mini, last week in Lagos.
The N97 Mini, expected to hit the Nigerian market next month, is targeted at users who want something more pocket-friendly while on the go. It comes with stainless steel accents and a sleek white casing. Additionally, it is available in a variety of colors and is affordable for end-users.
During the product unveiling in Lagos, the head of Nokia's research center, Henry Tirri, stated, "Customer feedback prompted us to introduce the Nokia N97 Mini. We have always been responsive to our customers' demands." He described the mobile phone as a software upgrade, combining everything Nokia is doing with technology, capable of serving as both a mobile phone and a laptop, providing computing power and accessories that make it unique compared to other phones with similar features.
"With approximately 32 gigabytes of memory and increased portability, the N97 Mini aims to offer convenience to users. The product's uniqueness lies in its customizable screen and ease of connection to LCD screens and Laser Jet printers," Tirri added.
The N97 Mini features a fully customizable home screen, a tilting 3.2" wide-screen touch display, the Symbian operating system, a full QWERTY keyboard, and 8 gigabytes, expandable to 24 gigabytes, of memory. Users will be able to browse the web quickly through a superfast 3G or Wi-Fi connection. They can also connect seamlessly with friends and favorite online services, receive live feeds from social networking sites, and access news services.
The white N97 Mini variant includes a 5-megapixel camera with Carl Zeiss Optics and dual LED flash, DVD-quality video recording, a music player, and email capabilities. The upgraded browser offers lifecasting, providing direct access to social networking sites from the home screen. Users can navigate easily with the A-GPS receiver and Nokia Maps. Furthermore, users can access a wide range of content from the Ovi Store, specializing in mobile phones and related product areas like laptops, mobile broadband, and accessories.
