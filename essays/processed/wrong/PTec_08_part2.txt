MWEB Nigeria GM John Ugbe said “Customers can now receive
incoming calls from any voice network including fixed telephone
lines and GSM lines. This will also enable our business customers
to send facsimile (fax) documents using conventional fax equipment
on the iWay VOIP line making business efficiencies smoother. Each
line activated on iWayVAP (voice appliance) will be assigned a
number and all calls originated from iWayVAP will display the
caller’s identity to enable customers to see beforehand who is
calling them”, he concluded.
