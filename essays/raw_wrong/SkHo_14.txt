How to make Soy milk
Healthy soy milk can be made easily at home. In this article we 
    show you how to make your own soy milk for a fraction of the cost 
    when compared to shop bought soy milk.
If you are making soy milk regularly then you may find 
    purchasing a automatic soy milk machine or soy milk maker will save 
    you a good deal of time.
Step 1 - soak the Soya beans
Soak your Soya beans overnight in a large bowl of water (for 
    almost eight to twelve hours).
Step 2 - crush the soaked beans
Put the soaked beans in your food blender and some amount of 
    water. Blend for a minute until all the beans are broken down.
Step 3 - strain the ground bean liquid
Sieve the concretion with a Muslim or cheese clothe, squeeze the 
    cloth so as much of the liquid is extracted as possible.
Step 4 - Boil the soya milk
Place the milk on fire and heat until the milk starts to boil. 
    Boil for about 5 minutes or till the milk stop foaming, then remove 
    the milk from the heat and allow to cool.
